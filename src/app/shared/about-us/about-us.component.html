Who is Ryanair?

Ryanair is Europe's largest low-cost airline, operating over 1,800 routes across 40+ countries. With a reputation for providing affordable and efficient travel, Ryanair has transformed the aviation industry, making air travel more accessible to millions of passengers every year. The airline continues to expand its network, offering budget-friendly flights to some of the most popular destinations worldwide.
